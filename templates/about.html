Description

Hello! I'm a third year Business Informatics student of HSE University and this web application is a part of my course work.

As you've already noticed the goal of the application is providing book recommendations for the readers.

I'm using a hybrid recommender system which combines content based and collaborative filtering approaches to achieve the given aim.

There are 4 types of recommendations:

  • recommendation based on my favourite book (by author);
  • recommendation based on my favourite book (by author and tag);
  • recommendation based on my preferences;
  • the most popular books list.

The first two of them are rested on content based approach (the first type uses the authors, the second one - the authors and the tags), the third one - on collaborative filtering and the last one doesn't use the recommender system at all (it's just an additional option).
